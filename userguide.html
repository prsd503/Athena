Admin Portal User Guide
Welcome to your new digital vehicle registry! This guide will help you manage your society’s vehicle records quickly and securely.

1. Getting Started

  • Access: Open your assigned Admin URL in your browser/app.
  • Login: Enter the credentials provided by the Owl Watch support team.
  • Security: Please keep your login details private. If you ever suspect unauthorized access, contact us immediately to reset your password.

2. Searching for a Vehicle

To quickly identify a vehicle owner or flat number, perform the following steps:

  • Enter the full Vehicle Number (e.g., GJ01AB1234) into the search box.
  • Click Search.

Actionable Results Framework:

  • WhatsApp: Click the green button to instantly message the owner about their vehicle.
  • Edit: Update flat details or mobile numbers if they change.
  • Delete: Remove records when a resident moves out.

3. Adding New Vehicles

Keep your database current by adding new arrivals immediately:

  • Navigate to the Add Vehicle section.
  • Enter the Vehicle Number, Flat/Name, Mobile Number, and Vehicle Type.
  • Click Save to Registry.

ℹ️ System Intelligence Note

The system will automatically notify you if a vehicle is already registered to prevent duplicate entries.

4. Bulk Operations (For System Admins)

You can manage hundreds of records at once using standardized CSV files.

  • Preparation: Download our pre-formatted CSV Template by clicking the "Download CSV Template" button. Open this file in Microsoft Excel or your choice of spreadsheet software, input your registry data, and save it back in .csv format.
  • Importing: Once your file is ready, click Bulk Import CSV. The system will securely scan for new records and automatically skip exact duplicates.

⚠️ Strict Data Quality & Pipeline Constraints

  • Formatting Address Records: Always use the exact same format structural baseline for Flat Numbers (e.g., use "A-101" systematically rather than mixing variants like "A101" or "A 101") to ensure consistent querying data maps.
  • Mobile Data: Ensure all registered mobile entries start with country code prefix 91.
  • Import Threshold limits: The processing system allows bulk imports of up to a maximum limit of **500 vehicle entries** at a time per batch upload.

5. Maintenance & Support

  • Data Backups: We highly recommend that you explicitly request a full data export from us periodically to maintain a local, offline version of your registry history records.
  • Advanced Requests: High-risk features like Bulk Data Deletion or Full Structural Data Exports are strictly managed directly by the specialized Owl Watch team to secure data integrity. Simply forward us an operational ticket request and our backend operators will complete the actions for you safely.
  • Regular Cleanup Cycles: We strongly advise performing a comprehensive structural "cleanup" of your platform database ecosystem every 6 months to clean and remove historical vehicles that no longer actively belong to your society boundaries.
